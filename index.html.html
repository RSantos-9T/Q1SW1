My favorite video game
Tower Defense Simulator
Tower defense Simulator is a defense type of game were you defend your tower using troops There are 4 normal gamemodes which are:
Easy mode

Easy mode is usually known as beginners mode and this is were tutorials happen (if i even remember)
Molten mode
Molten mode is known as a normal level that isnt that hard.

Intermediate mode
Intermediate mode is known as a challenge even to experienced players.

Fallen mode
Fallen mode is a very hard mode in TDS. Even the most experienced struggle their defense.

Hardcore mode
Hardcore is a much harder gamemode than fallen due to all upgrade prices are higher than any other gamemode. And its boss the Void Reaver has 10x more health than its predessesor the Fallen king.
Should you Play TDS? Absolutely its challenging while also fun and you get you play with your friends
